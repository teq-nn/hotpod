Session checklist

  • Host responsibilities: Ensure you are logged in with the host role and display the consent dialog before anyone joins.
  • Participant consent: Verbally reiterate that the session is being captured before starting to record or distribute tracks.
  • Safari support: Safari is currently unsupported for multitrack capture. Ask attendees to switch to a Chromium-based browser or Firefox.
  • Mixing limitations: This demo is for capture only. Plan to finish editing and mastering in an external DAW such as Logic Pro, Ableton Live, or Reaper.
